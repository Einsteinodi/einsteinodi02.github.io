ABOUT CEO

AIKHUELE EINSTEIN

Aikhuele Einstein is a nigerian national. Born and raised in Lagos, had his primary Education at "Saint Judes Primary Schools". Went on to finish his seconadry schooling at "FSTC IJEBU IMUSHIN" Ogun state.Got his Bsc in computer science from University of benin Edo state, Nigeria.

Art is something i love so much as a young kid. I was always curious about colors and how it works. Started off with corel draw 2012, editing and designing business logos. Took a while to acquire the skill but eventaully got the hack of it.

Nio arts was an idea i got during 2020 lock down. Frustated and emotionally downcast,a group of friends and i came up with an idea to network to as many as possible notifying art lovers about our services. Designing art and making the world a better place.
